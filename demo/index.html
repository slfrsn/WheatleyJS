WHEATLEY.JS

Click anywhere to place a portal. They're made with SASS flavoured CSS and just a smidge of javascript to handle the clicky bits!

How to use:

  • Left-click to place a blue portal
  • Right-click to place a orange portal
  • Left or right-click in a new location to move a portal
  • Click on a portal to destroy it
